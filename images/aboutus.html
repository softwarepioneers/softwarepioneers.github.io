Cloud Education System

Project

Education management information system (EMIS) is a national computing system to unify and manage private and public educational institutions to control all educational institutions in the homeland (Somaliland). And facilitate the tasks of the main government educational institutions. And they are: the Ministry of Education and the Ministry of Higher Education.

Cloud technologies help also cover a wider audience of students and manage their process of studying. Assess the tests, homework, projects taken by students, sending the feedback has never been that easy. Administrators, in their turn, can easily collaborate with one another and save money and time on the problem-solving process.
